What is the Sepolia Testnet?

The Sepolia testnet is an Ethereum test network designed for developers to test and deploy smart contracts before moving to the mainnet. It's faster and more reliable than older testnets like Rinkeby or Kovan.

Why Use This Faucet?

This faucet provides free Sepolia ETH for testing. It's helpful for developers building dApps, verifying transactions, and experimenting with DeFi or NFT projects. No wallet connection or registration required.

How to Request ETH

Simply enter your wallet address and click the "Request" button. Transactions are processed instantly. Make sure your wallet is on the Sepolia network.
